# Moderator Guide

## Day 0

1. Confirm eligibility and consent before observation.
2. Open `index.html?validation=1` with a clean research profile.
3. Do not explain the product. Ask: “你认为这个产品是做什么的？”
4. Ask the participant to enter one real matter in their natural wording.
5. Observe whether they find input, understand the draft, edit if needed, and confirm.
6. Ask them to compare the same matter in their normal tool.
7. Record confusion and interventions without copying full life text.

Do not lead the participant toward Commitment, Waiting For, or Next Best Action. Do not count moderator-directed reopening as retention.
