# Day 7 Interview

Ask open questions before showing event summaries:

1. What problem does 时刻 solve?
2. When did you choose to reopen it, and why?
3. Which real-life situations fit or did not fit?
4. Did you use Commitment, Waiting For, or Next Best Action?
5. Which system result was hardest to trust?
6. What required too much editing?
7. When was your previous tool better?
8. Would you continue, recommend it, or migrate more matters? Why?

Record categorized findings and evidence references. Do not invent quotations or reconstruct missing answers.
