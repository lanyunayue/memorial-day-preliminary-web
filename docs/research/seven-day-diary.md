# Seven-Day Diary

Use one short check-in per day. Do not require a product task.

- Did you open 时刻 voluntarily today?
- Did you record a real matter?
- Did any draft require correction?
- Did a suggestion help? Which feature category, without quoting the life text?
- Did an error reduce trust?
- Did you prefer your previous tool for anything?

Day 2 and Day 7 reopening count only when initiated by the participant for their own purpose. A prompted research session is recorded separately and is not retention.
