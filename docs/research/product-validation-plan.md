# PROJECT CHRONOS Product Validation Plan

## Decision

Test whether Chinese university students, interns, and early-career workers can turn their own fragmented life information into commitments, Waiting For items, and next actions with less effort than their current tool.

## Sample

- Minimum: 10 consenting participants.
- Preferred: 15-20 consenting participants.
- Duration: Day 0 observed first use, natural use on Day 1-6, Day 7 interview.
- Every participant uses their own wording and current comparison tool.

## Evidence

- Product exports contain only allowlisted local events and participant codes.
- Moderators record comprehension and draft usability on observation sheets stored outside this repository.
- Participant files are never committed to Git.
- Automated test fixtures are not research evidence.

## Status

The system and protocol are ready. Outcomes are **HUMAN PARTICIPANTS REQUIRED**.
