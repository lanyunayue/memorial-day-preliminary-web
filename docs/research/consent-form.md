# Participant Consent

Before enabling research recording, the moderator must explain:

- The study evaluates the product, not the participant.
- Participation is voluntary and may end at any time.
- The app works without research consent.
- The local log records event names, timing, counts, participant code, and session ID.
- The local log does not store full input text, name, phone, address, clipboard, browser history, passwords, tokens, or private keys.
- No remote analytics are used.
- The participant can view, export, delete, or stop recording.

Consent is valid only after the participant checks the explicit consent control and activates it with the assigned non-identifying code. Verbal silence or continued product use is not consent.
