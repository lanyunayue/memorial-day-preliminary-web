# Recruitment Message

We are testing “时刻”, a local-first web tool for organizing promises, things you are waiting for, and next actions from everyday Chinese sentences.

Participation takes one observed first-use session, natural optional use over seven days, a short daily check-in, and a Day 7 interview. Use only content you are comfortable entering. Do not enter passwords, identity numbers, private keys, confidential employer information, or sensitive third-party details.

Participation is voluntary. You may stop research recording at any time and continue using the product. Research events remain on your device until you choose to export them. Contact and scheduling details are managed outside the product export.
