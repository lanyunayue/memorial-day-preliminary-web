# Participant Profile

## Include

- Chinese university student, intern, or worker within two years of graduation.
- Regularly receives fragmented obligations through chat, verbal agreements, notices, or screenshots.
- Currently uses notes, calendar, task list, chat self-transfer, or memory to track them.
- Willing to use real personal wording for seven days.

## Exclude

- Project team members or people already trained on Chronos syntax.
- Anyone unable to provide informed consent.
- People whose participation depends on disclosing protected, employer-confidential, medical, financial, or credential data.

Recruit across different study and work contexts. Do not select only people already enthusiastic about the product.
